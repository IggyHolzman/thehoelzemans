Reception
Celebrate with us by the lake

After the ceremony, we invite you to join us for an evening of celebration overlooking the peaceful waters of Beaver Lake. Our reception will take place on a private estate surrounded by rolling hills, mature trees, and panoramic lake views that capture the quiet beauty of the Ozarks.

The property offers ample space for gathering, dining, dancing, and enjoying the sunset. This setting is meant to feel warm, natural, and inviting — a place where everyone can relax, celebrate, and share in the joy of the day.

Parking at the reception venue is easy and plentiful. Guests may park directly on the property, and attendants will help guide vehicles as needed. Unlike the ceremony location, there is no need to arrive early for parking — simply come ready to celebrate.

Throughout the evening, we will enjoy food, drink, music, and dancing. More details about the menu will be shared soon on the Food & Drink page as plans continue to take shape.

We will also be taking song requests for the reception playlist. If there’s a track that feels meaningful, fun, nostalgic, or simply perfect for the dance floor, please share it with us through the Two Jay Jukebox page.

This celebration is meant to feel personal and joyful — shaped not only by the setting, but by the people who fill it.

We hope you will take a moment to explore the rest of our wedding website as well. You can:

-read Our Story,

-test your knowledge with Jan & Jordon Trivia,

-browse photos and videos in HoelzMedia,

-preview the menu at Food & Drink, and

-leave a song request at the Two Jay Jukebox.

We can’t wait to celebrate with you!
