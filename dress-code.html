Dress Code
Garden Formal

Our wedding day takes place in the heart of the Ozarks, surrounded by the natural beauty of Eureka Springs and the peaceful waters of Beaver Lake. To honor both the setting and the occasion, the dress code is Garden Formal — a style that blends elegance with the soft, romantic feel of an outdoor celebration.

Garden Formal encourages polished attire with breathable fabrics, gentle colors, and silhouettes that feel refined without being overly rigid. Think of it as the sweet spot between traditional formalwear and the relaxed charm of a garden party.

For women: Dresses, jumpsuits, or coordinated sets in flowing fabrics, floral prints, pastels, or earth tones. Midi or maxi lengths are ideal. Heels, wedges, or dressy flats all work beautifully — choose what feels comfortable for walking on natural terrain.

For men: Suits or dress slacks with a blazer in lighter fabrics or colors. Ties are welcome but not required. Leather shoes or loafers are perfect; avoid overly casual footwear.

For everyone: Embrace the season. October in the Ozarks can be warm in the afternoon and cool by evening, so layers or shawls are encouraged. The goal is to feel elevated, comfortable, and ready to celebrate.

Thank you for helping to make our day special!
