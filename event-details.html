Event Details
Where, what and when

Nestled in the heart of the Ozarks, our ceremony will take place in the beautiful town of Eureka Springs, Arkansas where we are honored to celebrate the Sacrament of Holy Matrimony at St. Elizabeth of Hungary Catholic Church.

Our ceremony will begin at 1:00 PM on Saturday, October 10th, 2026, where we will exchange our vows and celebrate Mass. Guests are invited to bear witness as we receive the Sacrament.

Following the ceremony, the celebration continues in nearby Beaver Lake. The reception will include food, drink, and music, on 30 acres of private estate overlooking the lake.

The dress code for the day is Garden Formal to honor both the setting and the occasion. For your convenience, a detailed breakdown is available on the Dress Code page linked below.

Please note that this particular weekend will be lively throughout the region. Razorback football, and the Bikes, Blues & BBQ festival will be happening in nearby Fayetteville. Both events draw significant crowds, so to aid with lodging and travel, we have created a dedicated page on our site to help you find Where to Stay.
