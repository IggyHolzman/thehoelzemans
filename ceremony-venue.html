Ceremony
Come celebrate with us

Nestled in the heart of the Ozarks, our ceremony will take place in the beautiful town of Eureka Springs, Arkansas where we are honored to celebrate the Sacrament of Holy Matrimony at St. Elizabeth of Hungary Catholic Church.

Our ceremony will begin at 1:00 PM on Saturday, October 10th, 2026, where we will exchange our vows and celebrate Mass. Guests are invited to bear witness as we receive the Sacrament.

St. Elizabeth of Hungary Catholic Church is one of the most unique and beloved landmarks in Eureka Springs. Built directly into the hillside and connected to the Crescent Spring by a winding stone walkway, the church is known for its intimate courtyard, ivy‑covered walls, and the iconic bell tower that overlooks the historic district. Guests will enter through the garden pathway and gather inside the sanctuary, where stained glass, carved woodwork, and the soft glow of natural light create a peaceful and reverent setting for the Sacrament.

Because St. Elizabeth sits along a narrow historic street, parking is limited near the church. We encourage guests to arrive early and carpool when possible. Enjoy the views of the surrounding Ozark hills and church grounds before the ceremony begins.

Our wedding liturgy will be a full Catholic Mass, which includes prayers, readings, a homily, and the celebration of the Eucharist. All guests are warmly welcome to participate in all parts of the mass.

The dress code for the day is Garden Formal, chosen to complement both the sacred atmosphere of St. Elizabeth and the natural beauty of the Ozarks. See the Dress Code page linked below for more details.
